    • Brussels Planetarium Poetry Fest

    2024

    The 10th Brussels Planetarium Poetry Fest (November 28-30, 2024) blended poetry, immersive technology, and visual art, culminating in the final showcase of the Urban Travel Machines (UTM) Creative Europe project. The event transformed the Brussels Planetarium into a 360Â° storytelling space, featuring live performances by top European poets.

    Together with my colleague Ana Pinheiro, I created the animation "Em Nome do Centro", which was presented alongside a live performance by poet Modise Sekgothe, combining dynamic visuals with poetry for an immersive experience. The event was a collaboration between Aalto University (Finland), Royal Academy of Fine Arts Antwerp (Belgium), and Polish-Japanese Academy of Information Technology (Poland), coordinated by Philip Meersman and Tarja Nieminen.
