    • Wireless LED Suit System for Artistic Roller Skating (On Going)

    2024

    Designing a custom LED-embedded suit system for artistic roller skating on quad skates. The setup includes battery-powered components and a wireless control system capable of instantly transitioning LED states via radio signals from a master button. This system will simultaneously control 11 suits with real-time precision, laying the groundwork for future integration with MA2 lighting control systems.
