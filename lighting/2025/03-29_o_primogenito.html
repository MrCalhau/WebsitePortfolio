    • O Primogénito

    2025

    Synopsis:

    By abandoning the migratory route of the flock that avoids winter, an individual determined to reclaim his sense of wholeness sets out to face the hostility of the elements in order to penetrate the unforgiving wilderness. In doing so, he establishes a passage between the physical forest and the force of nature of an invisible spirit.

    Artistic residency with a final presentation as part of the Excentricidade program (Municipality of Guimarães).
