    I am a multimedia artist who graduated with a BA in Fine Arts from the Faculty of Fine Arts at the University of Porto. Born in Portugal, with both British and Portuguese origins, my interests span video, animation, sculpture, installation, and sound, but my true passion lies in deconstructing materials and technologies to pioneer alternative mediums and interfaces. I specialize in 3D printing, Blender software, modeling, rendering and programing, and I have created and published work for academic, research, professional incentives and events.
