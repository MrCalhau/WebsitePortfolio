    Hugo Aston is a multimedia artist who graduated with a BA in Fine Arts from the Faculty of Fine Arts at the University of Porto. Born in Portugal, with both British and Portuguese origins, his interests span video, animation, sculpture, installation, and sound, but his true passion lies in deconstructing materials and technologies to pioneer alternative mediums and interfaces. Specializing in 3D printing and motion, Hugo is an expert with Blender, modeling, rendering and programing, and has created and published work for academic, research, professional events and incentives.
