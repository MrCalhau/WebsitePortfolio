    • About
    I am a multimedia artist born in Portugal, with both British and Portuguese origins. My artistic interests span video, animation, sculpture, installation, and sound, but my true passion lies in deconstructing materials and technologies to pioneer alternative mediums and interfaces.
    To effectively realize my vision, I have honed a diverse skill set. I specialize in 3D printing, Blender software, modeling, rendering, and programming, and I have created and published work for academic research, professional initiatives, and events.
    I graduated with a BA in Fine Arts from the Faculty of Fine Arts at the University of Porto. Professionally, I have worked as a monitor/teacher, research member, spokesperson, graphic designer, multimedia assistant, and freelancer.
