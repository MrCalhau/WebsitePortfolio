    • In Time

    2023

    The primary goal of In Time was to serve as a technical milestone, demonstrating my mastery of Blender, particularly in its intended application for 3d. Developed over the course of a semester, this project allowed me to focus on refining my skills and integrating all the knowledge I had gathered up to that point. The technical focus included modeling, texturing, rigging, animating, and sound design.
